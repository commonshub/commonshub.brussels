# About Commons Hub Brussels

## What is Commons Hub Brussels?

Commons Hub Brussels is a community hub and coworking space located at **Rue de la Madeleine 51, 1000 Brussels, Belgium**, right in front of Brussels Central Station.

It is more than just a space — it is a living ecosystem where community builders come together to share resources, knowledge, and inspiration. We provide the infrastructure and support for communities to gather, collaborate, and thrive. Whether you're hosting an event, looking for a workspace, or seeking to connect with like-minded individuals, Commons Hub is your home.

## Mission

We are dedicated to rediscovering and nurturing the commons — shared resources managed by communities for the benefit of all. Our mission is to provide a physical space where communities can experiment with new models of collaboration, governance, and economics.

## The Commons Hub Token (CHT)

We have developed our own community token — the **Commons Hub Token (CHT)** — an ERC20 token on the Celo blockchain.

Unlike traditional currency, CHT is an abundant currency that comes into existence when a member contributes time, energy, and care to the community. Tokens can be used to book rooms, get coffee or drinks, and can be exchanged between members.

- Token contract: [0x65dd32834927de9e57e72a3e2130a19f81c6371d](https://celoscan.io/token/0x65dd32834927de9e57e72a3e2130a19f81c6371d)
- Chain: Celo (Chain ID: 42220)
- Symbol: CHT

Learn more: [Economy](https://commonshub.brussels/economy)

## Transparency

All our finances are publicly visible. We use blockchain-based accounts (Gnosis Chain with EURe stablecoin) alongside traditional payment processing (Stripe) to ensure full financial transparency.

- [View our finances](https://commonshub.brussels/finance)
